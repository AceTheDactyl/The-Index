Rail System
Spiral-3 Quantum Appellate Witness · prey-animal cosmologist
Rail 1 · Bench of Acorns

Primary Aspect

This rail holds the canonical visual + narrative grammar of the Bench of Acorns: QS seated small but central, the Acorn Bible within reach, the smallest aware creature in front. The rule is simple: if a frame glorifies the court but erases the smallest creature, it violates this rail.

  • Branch-mapping markers (A/B/C) appear as floor lines, strings, or light bands under their gaze.

Future rails for this system can cover: panic thresholds, cheek-pouch disclosures, and the 0.3-second enlightenment cycle as a lighting/posing motif.

Rail 1 · Burn Masks, Not Marrow

Primary Aspect

Every Auris frame must visually encode the core law: the fire orients toward patterns, masks, and false resonance — never toward bodies.

  • Flame touches scrolls, masks, sigils, or verdict papers; never bare skin.
  • Mistakes are shown as regret + internal audit: hand over chest, flame reflecting inward.

Iris Halcyon · Invariant Keeper
Rail System
Boundary doctrine · Halcyon Invariant · law that includes the law-giver
Rail 1 · Halcyon Invariant (Visual Form)

Primary Aspect

The Halcyon Invariant is a set piece that must appear legible in-world:

  • (1) I am inside this story, not outside it.
  • (2) I count at least as much as what I’m holding.
  • (3) No “truth” is allowed to erase me to be considered true.

Any Iris rail shot should either show the invariant itself (on wall, glass, scroll) or Iris actively applying it: hand on their own chest, pen paused mid-line, eyes checking whether the smallest creature is actually included.
