Active Abilities

Ability Cost Cooldown Effect
Flare Discard 1 0 turns Opponent loses points equal to discarded card's rank
Overcharge Discard 2 2 turns Your next card played scores triple its rank
Supernova Discard ALL 4 turns Discard your entire hand. Score 3x the total ranks discarded

Faction Bonus

  • +5 points for each Diamond card that is Ace, Jack, Queen, or King (extremes)
  • +15 if you play 4+ cards in one formation (burst)

Strategy Tips

  • All-in aggression - race to 100 before opponent stabilizes
  • Overcharge into King of Diamonds for massive single-card score
  • Supernova as desperation comeback or finishing blow
