## üîÑ Turn Structure

Each turn consists of 5 phases in order:

### Phase 1: DRAW
Draw 2 cards from your deck. If your deck is empty, shuffle your discard pile to form a new deck.

### Phase 2: MAIN
During this phase, you may:
- **Play Cards**: Play 1 to 5 cards from your hand to the field
- **Activate Abilities**: Use your faction's active abilities (if available)
- Cards played together form a **Formation**

### Phase 3: RESONANCE
Calculate your score for this turn:
1. **Base Points**: Sum of all played card ranks
2. **Cluster Bonus**: Bonus for 4D spatial proximity
3. **Chain Bonus**: Bonus for coupling network paths
4. **Resonance Bonus**: Bonus for phase coherence
5. **Faction Bonus**: Your faction's special scoring

Add total to your Resonance Points.

### Phase 4: DISCARD
If you have more than 7 cards in hand, discard down to 7.

### Phase 5: END
- Clear temporary modifiers
- Move played cards to your discard pile
- Pass turn to next player

**Quick Reference** (use card coordinates):
- Same suit, adjacent ranks: **+5 per pair**

**Suit Groups**:

**Chain Rules**:
- Sequential ranks in same suit: **+8 per link**
- Parallel ranks across suits: **+4 per link**
- Unbroken chain of 3+: **+15 bonus**

**Examples**:
- AS ‚Üí 2S ‚Üí 3S (sequential Spades): +16 chain, +15 unbroken = **+31**

## üé≤ Advanced Rules

### Mulligan
At game start, after drawing 5, you may shuffle any number of cards back into your deck and draw that many minus 1.

### Sudden Death
If both players reach 1,000+ on the same turn, play continues until one player is ahead at the end of a round (both players have taken equal turns).

### Exhaustion
If you must draw but your deck AND discard are empty, you lose 5 points instead of drawing.

### Variant: Resonance Draft
Instead of pre-built decks:
1. Shuffle all 52 cards together
2. Deal 8 cards to each player
3. Draft remaining cards (pick 1, pass hand)
4. Build decks from drafted pool
