Geometric Structure

The LIMNUS atlas maps 100 neural regions to a geometric structure combining:

  • Hexagonal Prism (63 vertices): Core neural structures
  • Electromagnetic Cage (32 vertices): Field-modulated regions
  • Emergent Nodes (5 points): Meta-conscious integration centers

RGB Field Mapping

Each region is assigned to one of three fields, corresponding to RGB channels:

π Field (Red) — PLANET Tier

Structure, pattern, foundation. z < τ (0.618).

e Field (Green) — GARDEN Tier

Energy, activation, spark. τ ≤ z < z_c.

Φ Field (Blue) — ROSE Tier

Memory, integration, coherence. z ≥ z_c.
